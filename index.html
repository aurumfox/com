About Our Meme Coin AURUM FOX (AFOX)

Welcome to the world of AURUM FOX! We are a new, fully decentralized meme coin, born from the community and for the community. Our goal is to create a fun, dynamic, and truly people-powered meme coin on the Solana blockchain.

AURUM FOX is a meme coin depicted as a golden coin featuring a cute, cartoon fox and the text "AURUM FOX." Its name combines the Latin "Aurum" (gold), which symbolizes value, and "Fox," a popular image used to create viral brands in the meme coin space.

We believe in transparency and fairness. All Mint Authority (ability to create new meme coins) and Freeze Authority (ability to freeze existing meme coins) have been permanently revoked. This ensures a fixed total supply and guarantees your assets cannot be locked. No one, not even the creators, can mint new meme coins or interfere with your holdings. AURUM FOX (AFOX) is a 100% community-driven meme coin.
