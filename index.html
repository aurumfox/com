About Our AURUM FOX (AFOX) Meme Token

Welcome to the world of **AURUM FOX!** We are a new, fully decentralized meme token, built by the community and for the community. Our goal is to create a fun, dynamic, and truly community-governed meme token on the Solana blockchain.
